Complete Nutrition Guide

Optimizing your diet for every stage of your journey.

Weeks 1–13

Organ Foundation

Focus on Folate (Vitamin B9) and B6 to support neural tube development and manage early pregnancy nausea. Quantity isn't key yet; quality is.

  • 🌱 Leafy Greens (Spinach, Kale)
  • 🥚 Eggs (Choline for brain)
  • 🍋 Ginger & Citrus (Nausea relief)
Weeks 14–26

Bone & Blood Health

The "Golden Phase." Baby's skeleton is hardening, and your blood volume expands significantly. Prioritize Calcium and Iron.

  • 🥩 Lean Red Meat / Legumes (Iron)
  • 🥛 Dairy / Fortified Milk (Calcium)
  • 🍌 Bananas & Nuts (Magnesium)
Weeks 27–40+

Energy & Preparation

Rapid growth phase. Baby needs fuel for fat layer development. Switch to small, frequent, high-fiber, nutrient-dense meals.

  • 🥑 Avocado & Olive Oil (Healthy Fats)
  • 🍐 Pears & Berries (Fiber)
  • 🥦 Broccoli (Vitamin K for clotting)
CRITICAL SAFETY

The "Never" List

To protect your pregnancy, completely avoid these foods due to high risk of bacterial contamination or mercury.

  • 🛑 Unpasteurized Dairy (Soft Cheeses)
  • 🛑 Raw Sprouts (Alfalfa, Clover)
  • 🛑 High-Mercury Fish (Shark, Swordfish)
  • 🛑 Raw/Rare Meat & Eggs
